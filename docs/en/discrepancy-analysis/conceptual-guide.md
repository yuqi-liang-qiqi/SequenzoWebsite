# Discrepancy Analysis Conceptual Guide

Discrepancy analysis studies whether sequence trajectories differ across groups based on pairwise sequence dissimilarities.

At a high level, it decomposes total discrepancy into between-group and within-group components and evaluates:

- how much trajectory variation is associated with group labels;
- where differences occur along sequence positions;
- whether observed differences are larger than expected by chance via permutation.
